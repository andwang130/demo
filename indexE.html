Company profile

Sichuan Chuangkang Highway Pianning Survey and Design Co.,Ltd was restructured for Ya'an Highway Design Institute, Qualifications include Grade B of Engineering Survey (Geotechnical Engineering, Engineering Survey), Grade B of Highway Industry (Highway) and Grade B of Municipal Industry (Bridge Engineering, Road Engineering). (Survey certificate: B251014610, design certificate: A151014613, A251014610). It is an engineering and technical backbone enterprise with strong engineering investigation and design, engineering management and planning consultation capabilities. The company's development goals and plans are: Depending on its own technological and location advantages, it actively develops the highway survey and design market in central and Western Sichuan and Tibet, strives to become a medium-sized survey and design unit with good reputation, high quality and high efficiency, and contributes to the construction of the Western Transportation hub. The company has 55 employees, including 45 professional and technical personnel (12 senior titles, 20 intermediate titles and 13 junior titles). It has more than 10 offices, including General Office of Industry, Comprehensive Planning Office, One Highway, Two Highways, Three Highways, Bridge and Tunnel Office, Municipal Office, Budget Office, Geotechnical Room, Later Service Center, Operating Office, Office and Finance Department. It has total station, Gps-RTK satellite dynamic positioning system, digitizer and other advanced survey and design instruments. More than 20 sets of Surveying and designing software, including advanced highway, bridge, rock and soil, budget estimate and so on, are available. Now the computer network has been realized, the database resources are shared, the rate of computer drawing and drawing is 100%, and the capacity of Highway Survey and design is 2000 km/year. Enterprise Service Purpose: Deeply understand the essence of quality management, take the continuous improvement of design works as the core work, and provide customers with high quality, efficient and comprehensive service in the whole process.
