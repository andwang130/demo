Company news

1. The company was commended by excellent design units in Tibet.

At the beginning of the new year, good news came from Tibet. The company was commended by excellent design units in 2017. The general manager of the company went to collect the certificate in person. This is a full affirmation of the quality of the company's survey and design work, greatly inspiring the confidence of all staff and workers, but also put forward higher requirements for future work.

2. The company holds shareholders'and workers' meetings

After the Spring Festival, the company held successive shareholder meetings and staff meetings to summarize the work in 2017 and arrange for the deployment of various work in 2018. In addition to participating in market competition and expanding project sources, the company will also complete the following tasks in 2018: organizing and completing the application materials for postponement of highway design qualification to ensure the approval of examination; introducing strategic investment to enhance the company's management level; strengthening the management of branches; new declaration and consultation qualification; and speeding up with major industries. Horizontal cooperation of the design institute, acceleration of joint efforts with high-quality construction units, and preparation for the next step into the EPC market.

3. The company won the bid for Leshan project

On March 12, 2018, the company won the bid for the reconnaissance and design of the first-class highway overhaul project of Wutong Bridge Section of 213 National Highway of Leshan City. The project is time-intensive and disturbing. The company will strengthen the staffing of the project team and complete the work with high quality and efficiency.

4.The company undertakes the project of China Electricity Construction Group

On April 22, 2018, entrusted by China Electric Power Construction Group, the company undertook the Yuyanghe Reservoir Project traffic bridge, 420 meters long and 23 meters wide. The main span of the bridge is a special-shaped continuous structure, which needs to be combined with the structure of the dam and sluice.

5. The company organizes and completes the field acceptance and design review of the Tibet project.

From April to May 2018, the company dispatched several project teams to Tibet to complete the acceptance and acceptance of the winning project in 2017, and stationed in Zhalasa. On-site examination and revision of drawings were completed in a timely manner, which received unanimous praise from owners at all levels, and guaranteed the start of construction of the project this year.

6. The Company Wins the Bid for Henan Project

In June, the company won the bid for nearly 1000 kilometers of rural road design project package in Henan Province, which is the company's first successful bid in Henan Province.

7. The company successively wins the bid day-to-day project

In June 2018, the company successively won the bid for the reconnaissance and design of the reconstruction and expansion projects of Yuxin Road and Lisan Road in Tianquan County, which consolidated the traditional market.

8. The company organizes training for new graduates

In July 2018, the company organized this year's new graduate training. This year's graduates mainly come from Chongqing Jiaotong University and Southwest University, majoring in highway and bridge. After short-term training, freshmen are assigned to all departments and quickly participate in project learning practice.

9. The Company Wins the Bid for Tibet Project

On August 15, 2018, the company won the bid for the seventh section of the 2018 rural highway survey and design in Naqu, Tibet. The total mileage of the project is nearly 300 kilometers. It requires that all the work be completed within two months and the construction start at the end of the year.

10. The company completes the field work of the Tibet project

By mid-September 2018, the company has carefully organized to overcome various difficulties such as altitude (up to 5,000 meters), climate, distance and so on. It has successfully completed all field work, launched collaborative design synchronously, and guaranteed maximum efficiency to meet the owner's deadline requirements.

11. The Company Wins the Bid for the Guangyuan Project

On September 25, 2018, the company successfully won the bid for reconstructing the highway from Wangjiazhen to Wencun Township, Zhaohua District, Guangyuan City. The project is 17 kilometers of third-class highway.

12. The company organized the completion of the investigation of the damaged section of National Highway 549 in 2018

During this year's flood season, Shimian County in Ya'an City suffered many years of rare floods. The new national highway 549 was damaged by large floods, debris flows and landslides, which had a serious impact on highway route layout. After the flood, according to the owner's request, the company quickly organized technical personnel to carry out detailed investigation of the disaster along the line to provide support for the next reasonable optimization and adjustment of the section scheme.

13. The company compiled and completed the National Standard "Technical Regulations for Highway Corrugated Steel Culverts"

In September, the company organized technical experts to compile the "Technical Regulations for Highway Corrugated Steel Culverts" and put forward reasonable suggestions on the use of various indicators of highway corrugated steel culverts and the anti-corrosion of highway corrugated steel in Alpine permafrost areas, based on the company's design experience in Tibet's high altitude areas, and absorbed into the new specifications. The Code is edited by the Second Public Court of China Communications.
